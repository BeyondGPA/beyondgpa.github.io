BeyondGPA is a data-driven project developed as part of the INF8808 Data Visualization course at Polytechnique Montréal. The project delves into the complex relationship between academic performance and career success, aiming to provide actionable insights for students, educators, and professionals.

Using a dataset of 5000 students, BeyondGPA examines how factors such as GPA, internships, university ranking, and networking influence career outcomes like job offers, starting salaries, and career satisfaction. The project challenges the traditional notion that academic success alone determines professional achievement.

By focusing on a holistic approach to career development, BeyondGPA encourages individuals to consider the broader spectrum of skills and experiences that contribute to long-term success and fulfillment.
